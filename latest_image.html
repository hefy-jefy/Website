Latest Image - Moon / Mars animation January 30th 

"What we need is Star Peace not Star Wars" ~ Mikhail Gorbachev

On January 30th the Moon's orbit appeared very close to the planet Mars.  Depending on your location it may have actually occluded Mars, in my part of California it came very close.  The animation is a series of 44 images taken over a period of about 2.5 hours on the evening of January 30th.
