Hardware and software

If the James Webb infra-red telescope was mounted on earth it would be able to detect a bumble-bee on the moon.

Hardware:

  •  Orion ED80T CF Triplet APO Refractor, focal length 480mm.

  •  Orion Sirius EQ-G Computerized GoTo Telescope Mount.

  •  Canon 100D DSLR full spectrum camera.

  •  Orion field flattener.

  •  Astronomix UV-IR filter.

  •  Orion 60mm Guide Scope, focal length 240mm.

  •  Orion Starshoot Auto Guider.

  •  Green laser pointer.

  •  Home made Peltier cooler for camera.

  •  Home made dew shields for telescope and guide scope.

  •  Home made controller for camera cooling and temp/humidity data.

Acquisition software:​

  • Backyard EOS camera control and logging

  • Open PHD2 Guiding

  • Stellarium

  • EQMOD telescope control interface with Stellarium


Processing software​:

  • Siril pre-process/process.

  • Deep Sky Stacker.

  • Photoshop CS3

  • Starnet ++

  • RC Astro GradientXterminator
