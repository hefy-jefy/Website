Latest Image - The North American Nebula and the Pelican Nebula

"What we need is Star Peace not Star Wars" ~ Mikhail Gorbachev

Another nebula consisting of a cloud of Hydrogen gas being illuminated by a bright star. However in this case the star is not visible as it is hidden by the dark cloud of dust in the center of the image.  It seemed appropriate that I acquired this image on Thanksgiving 2022, conditions were good with clear skies and no moon.
