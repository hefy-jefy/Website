Latest Image - The California Nebula

"What we need is Star Peace not Star Wars" ~ Mikhail Gorbachev

The California Nebula is named for its resemblance to the US State of California.  The bright star is Menkib, a very luminous Supergiant star in the constellation Perseus. The Nebula and Menkib are about 1200 light years away from Earth. They are about 200 light years apart.  It is the intense radiation from Menkib that causes the hydrogen gas in the Nebula to glow.
